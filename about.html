About The Conch Paradox

The Book

The Conch Paradox emerged from the intersection of personal crisis and professional insight. This book weaves together personal narrative, economic analysis, and natural philosophy.

The Author

Rendell de Kort is a PhD researcher in development economics focusing on economic resilience in Small Island Developing States (SIDS), with specific focus on the Dutch Caribbean.

Professional Background:

  • Partner at Cornerstone Economics
  • World Bank Consultant (Disaster Risk Financing)
  • University of Aruba (Dutch Caribbean Digital Competence Network (DCDC-Network))
  • Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam (PhD Candidate)
